Our Current Mission

Sophie, aka Sweetpea

We have now made it to our fifth mission! Meet Sophie, aka Sweetpea! Sophie has had a hard life in terms of finding a forever home. CrisisDogsNC (for more information visit https://www.crisisdogsnc.org/) has been kind enough to pay for her care until they are able to find a stable foster home for her. Our goal is to raise $400 to help CrisisDogsNC continue to support Sophie until she finds a home. For more information on Sophie, or to adopt her, visit https://crisisdogsnc.org/dog/sweet-pea/.

Our mission is to help other animals like Sophie, whether that be with their medical or shelter needs. You can help us reach our goal and make a difference in Sophie's life by purchasing our rings or donating directly to her cause. 100% of the profits made by our rings will go towards animals in need, currently focusing on Sophie.

How You Can Help Change the World

  • 1
    Support
    You can support Halo Paws and the animals that we are funding in many ways! One way is thorugh donating! You can also tell others about our non-profit. We care and value every one of our donors. There are many ways to donate money including by purchasing our rings or by donating directly to the animals! Every penny of our profit goes towards saving animals in need. Every action that you make to support our non-profit will not go unseen, and is making an immense difference in the lives of the animals we are supporting.
  • 2
    Donate
    Along with purchasing our rings, you can always donate! Donating is one of the best ways to help make a difference for our animals! Every penny counts and each donation or purchase means more to us and the animals than you could every imagine!
  • 3
    Spread the Word
    Tell your friends, family, and others about our non-profit! This is one of the best ways that you can help make a difference in the lives of animals! By raising awareness about our cause, we can get more support and funding for the animals in need. As Ryonosuke Satoro said, "Individually, we are one drop. Together, we are an ocean."
